Refund Policy – Kolet SaaS Platform

1. Eligibility for Refund
Customers are eligible for a refund within 14 days of the initial subscription purchase if they are not satisfied with the service.

2. Refund Conditions
Refunds are only granted for first-time subscriptions. Renewals are non-refundable unless there is a proven billing error.

3. Billing Errors
If a customer believes they were charged incorrectly, they must contact support within 7 days of the charge. The billing team will investigate and respond within 3 business days.

4. Processing Time
Approved refunds are processed within 5-10 business days depending on the customer's bank.

5. Non-Refundable Cases
Refunds are not provided for:
- Partial subscription usage
- Downgrades after the billing cycle has started
- Failure to cancel before renewal

6. Enterprise Contracts
Enterprise customers have custom refund clauses defined in their signed agreements.
