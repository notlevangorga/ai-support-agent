Escalation & SLA Policy – Kolet SaaS Platform

1. Standard Support Response Times
Basic Plan: 48 business hours
Pro Plan: 24 business hours
Enterprise Plan: 4 business hours

2. Critical Incidents
Critical incidents include:
- System outage
- Data breach
- Payment processing failure affecting multiple users

Critical incidents must be escalated immediately to the on-call engineer.

3. Escalation Procedure
Level 1: Customer Support Agent
Level 2: Technical Support Specialist
Level 3: Engineering Team
Level 4: Head of Engineering

4. SLA Guarantee
Enterprise customers receive 99.9% uptime guarantee.
If uptime falls below SLA threshold, service credits may apply.

5. Legal Complaints
Any legal threat or compliance issue must be escalated directly to the Legal Department within 2 hours.
